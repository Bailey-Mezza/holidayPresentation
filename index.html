Explore the top destinations and unforgettable activities on this Greek island paradise.

Catch a stunning sunset and enjoy traditional tavernas in this picturesque mountain village.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Tree of Hippocrates: Legendary tree where Hippocrates is said to have taught medicine.
  • Take a boat tour to nearby islands: Nisyros and Kalymnos are just a short trip away.
  • Relax in thermal springs: Therma Beach offers natural hot springs right by the sea.
  • Enjoy local cuisine: Try fresh seafood, moussaka, and local wines at seaside tavernas.

Paradise Beach

Paradise Beach is one of the most popular beaches on Kos, known for its soft white sand, shallow turquoise waters, and water sports. It's ideal for families, sun-seekers, and snorkelers alike. Don’t miss the inflatable water park in the summer!

Ancient Agora

The Ancient Agora of Kos is a fascinating archaeological site in the heart of Kos Town. Once a bustling marketplace, it now features well-preserved ruins of temples, baths, columns, and mosaic floors. It's a must-visit for history lovers exploring the island’s classical heritage.

Zia Village

Zia is a charming village nestled in the Dikeos mountains, offering stunning views of the sunset and authentic Greek cuisine. Don't miss the handmade local crafts and traditional music!
