Things to Do

  • Visit the Tree of Hippocrates: Legendary tree where Hippocrates is said to have taught medicine.
  • Take a boat tour to nearby islands: Nisyros and Kalymnos are just a short trip away.
  • Relax in thermal springs: Therma Beach offers natural hot springs right by the sea.
  • Enjoy local cuisine: Try fresh seafood, moussaka, and local wines at seaside tavernas.

Zia Village

Zia is a charming village nestled in the Dikeos mountains, offering stunning views of the sunset and authentic Greek cuisine. Don't miss the handmade local crafts and traditional music!
