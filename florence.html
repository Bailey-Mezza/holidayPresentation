Why Florence?

Florence is the heart of the Renaissance — full of art, history, and stunning architecture like the Duomo and Ponte Vecchio.

  • 🎨 Uffizi Gallery & Michelangelo’s David
  • 🍝 Delicious Tuscan food & gelato
  • 🚶 Walkable and full of charm
